About Us

The story behind every frame

OUR STORY

A Passion for Visual Storytelling

Founded in 2010 by Alexander Reid, LensCraft Studio began as a small downtown loft with a simple yet powerful vision: to redefine photography by blending artistic mastery with heartfelt storytelling. What started as a one-person passion project has blossomed into one of New York's premier photography studios.

Today, our team of 15+ skilled professionals brings together diverse expertise spanning wedding, portrait, fashion, commercial, and event photography. We've had the privilege of working with over 500 clients, each project fueling our commitment to creative excellence.

Every image we create is more than a photograph — it's a visual narrative that captures the essence of a moment, the spirit of a person, or the soul of a brand. We believe that extraordinary photography has the power to inspire, connect, and transform.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones & Growth

2010

Founded LensCraft Studio in a small downtown loft with a bold vision to redefine photography through artistic storytelling and emotional depth.

2013

Expanded to a 3,000 sq ft state-of-the-art studio and grew the team to 8 creative professionals, adding fashion and commercial photography services.

2016

Won 'Best Photography Studio' at the National Creative Arts Awards, establishing LensCraft as an industry leader in wedding and portrait photography.

2019

Launched our aerial and drone photography division, reached 1,000+ completed projects, and introduced cinematic videography services.

2022

Opened our second studio location, introduced AI-enhanced editing workflow, and expanded our team to 15+ creative professionals.

2024

Recognized as a Top 10 Photography Studio nationwide with 2,500+ projects completed and 35+ industry awards to our name.
